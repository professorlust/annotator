  • Quadratic Weighted Kappa: a measure of inter-rater agreement between two raters that provide discrete numeric ratings. Potential values range from -1 (representing complete disagreement) to 1 (representing complete agreement). A kappa value of 0 is expected if all agreement is due to chance.
